René P.

is the founder of ActSecure. Before starting ActSecure, he had provided technical assistance and training to local organizers on his own for the last 3 years. Currently, he is also the IT Manager for the New Economy Coalition, a network of organizations imagining and building a future where people, communities, and ecosystems thrive. He was also deeply involved with the Occupy Boston Movement, organizing with the Accounting, Tech, Media, and Logistics working groups in 2011. In his spare time… he doesn't have any of this. However, he has seen every episode of Mr Robot.

Alex S.

is an activist, technologist and agent of social change. His interests are in exploring issues in community-building, social justice, education and leveraging technology to address social problems. During the day he's a Technology teacher with Boston Public Schools and a mentor at Resilient Coders.

Matthew K.

is an IT and security veteran with 20+ years experience in the industry. Matthew's experience includes co-founding an internet company before Netscape was a company (EmeraldNET, in 1994), helping start a new airline (National Airlines) by designing the ENTIRE IT infrastructure (call centers, to web sites to data centers to ops crews) and managing the 17,000+ node Ask.com search systems cluster. Since 2011 Matthew has been a stay-at-home dad and activist participating in the Occupy movement, as well as serving as the National Technology Director for Restore the 4th. In his spare time, Matthew enjoys smashing fascists.

Lizzy J.

is an organizer and trainer who is passionate about making digital security accessible to everyone, and lifting up the voices and needs of Queer, Trans, and non binary people of color and the poor and working class. They are the founder of counter-surveillance group Privacy Watch in St. Louis, co-director and founder of the Deep Abiding Love Project training collective, and are currently the Surveillance and Security campaigner for internet rights group Fight for the Future. In their personal life, Lizzy likes to read stories about anti-capitalist revolution to their cats, knit things without patterns, and loves TNG the best of all star treks.
